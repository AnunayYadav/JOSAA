About Admission Explorer

Simplifying college admissions exploration for JoSAA, CSAB, JAC Chandigarh, JAC Delhi, UPTAC, and GGSIPU.

Created By

Developed with ❤️ by Anunay Yadav.

The Purpose

Navigating through hundreds of pages of PDF cut-off lists during counselling is slow and exhausting. Admission Explorer aggregates opening and closing ranks from all rounds of JoSAA, CSAB, JAC Chandigarh, JAC Delhi, UPTAC, and GGSIPU into a single, high-performance web interface. Students can easily filter colleges matching their rank range, check quotas, and explore possibilities in seconds.

Key Capabilities

  • JoSAA Mode: Ranks for IITs, NITs, IIITs, and GFTIs.
  • CSAB Mode: Special round vacancy details for NIT+ system.
  • JAC Chandigarh Mode: Ranks for local institutes (UIET, CCET, CCA).
  • JAC Delhi Mode: Ranks for top technical institutes in Delhi (DTU, NSUT, IIITD, IGDTUW).
  • UPTAC Mode: Ranks for technical colleges in Uttar Pradesh.
  • GGSIPU Mode: Cut-offs for Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University affiliated engineering colleges.
  • Google Search on Click: Fast lookup of institute details directly from the table or predictor cards.
