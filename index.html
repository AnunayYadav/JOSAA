About Admission Explorer

Simplifying college admissions exploration for JoSAA, CSAB, and JAC Chandigarh.

Created By

Developed with ❤️ by Anunay Yadav.

The Purpose

Navigating through hundreds of pages of PDF cut-off lists during counselling is slow and exhausting. Admission Explorer aggregates opening and closing ranks from all rounds of JoSAA, CSAB, and JAC Chandigarh into a single, high-performance web interface. Students can easily filter colleges matching their rank range, check quotas, and explore possibilities in seconds.

Key Capabilities

  • JoSAA Mode: Ranks for IITs, NITs, IIITs, and GFTIs.
  • CSAB Mode: Special round vacancy details for NIT+ system.
  • JAC Chandigarh Mode: Ranks for local institutes (UIET Chandigarh, CCET, CCA, UIET Hoshiarpur).
  • Google Search on Click: Fast lookup of institute details directly from the table.
